Midwest Aquifers by Square Decimal Degrees


Link to high resolution map

Authored by: Ryan Lark

Only aquifers with area greater than 1 square decimal degree were deemed "major aquifers" and labeled on the map.

 Software Used: QGIS 3.14 and Windows 10. Data was sourced from the U.S. Geological Survey. CRS used for map was EPSG: 2163.
